Fruit and Yogurt Smoothie

Home

Description

A very simple, quick and delicious way to refresh your day with a creamy, fruity drink we all love. Try swapping in fruits and juices to your own preference!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup yogurt
  • ¼ cup pineapple juice
  • 1 ½ teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

Steps

  1. Combine strawberries, banana, yogurt, pineapple juice, sugar, orange juice, and milk in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
